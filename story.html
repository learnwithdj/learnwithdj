My Story

The journey that led to “Learn with DJ”

Learning by Doing

How It All Began

Hello Learner,
I’m Dinesh J — you can call me DJ.

In my final semester, I graduated top of my class and landed a software developer role at Amazon before even walking the stage. Around then, a few of my friends who’d headed to U.S. master’s programs reached out—they were buried under assignments and struggling to grasp core concepts. I jumped in: breaking down problems, sketching out examples, and making the abstract feel concrete.

Word got around fast. One friend told another, and soon I was helping small study groups across time zones. What began as casual tutoring turned into a steady stream of students looking for clear, reliable guidance. By November 2022, supporting grad students had become my passion project.

A New Challenge: The Rise of AI

Then came late 2022 and the ChatGPT boom. AI can be a powerful learning aid—but without a solid foundation, it often misfires, leaving students with half‑baked or even incorrect answers—and sometimes in hot water for accidental plagiarism.

Why I Created Learn with DJ

That’s why I built LearnwithDJ: to offer expert‑crafted, concept‑first solutions. Submit your toughest coursework through Get Help, and my team of human experts will guide you step by step—no AI guesswork, no shortcuts. Our mission is to help you truly understand, excel in your studies, and build confidence for your career.

So, wake up, stay curious, and Start Learning!

Wishing you all the best.
— DJ
