Why AI Hallucination Happens

AI-generated content often sounds fluent but can sometimes include made-up facts or fake citations. This is known as AI hallucination.

How to Tackle It

  • Always double-check references.
  • Use AI only as a writing assistant, not as the final source.
  • Understand the concepts and rewrite in your own words.
